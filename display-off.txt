To verify the name of the display, run
xrandr --query
it should be on the first few lines, right before connected and the display resolution.
In the examples below, the display is called HDMI-A-1

TO TURN DIPLAY OFF:
WAYLAND_DISPLAY=wayland-0 wlopm --off HDMI-A-1

TO TURN DISPLAY ON:
WAYLAND_DISPLAY=wayland-0 wlopm --on HDMI-A-1
